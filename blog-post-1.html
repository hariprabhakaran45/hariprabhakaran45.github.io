How I Built My Portfolio

Published: June 2025

Creating my personal portfolio was an exciting challenge. I wanted a clean, modern, and mobile-first design that reflects both my technical skills and creativity.

I started with a design reference from a Canva/Webflow-style template and recreated it using pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I used a dark theme to give it a bold look, paired with soft animations and a clean font.

One of my favorite parts was implementing a project section with right-aligned preview images and responsive layouts. It taught me a lot about flexbox and grid combinations.

I kept performance in mind — optimizing images and reducing layout shifts. I also made it easy to extend by adding more sections like Blog, Contact Form, and GitHub/LinkedIn links.

If you're a beginner building your own, start small. Then iterate, tweak styles, and publish your site using GitHub Pages or Netlify. It’s the best way to show your skills!
